Dataset and Preprocessing#

In this section we describe all the datasets used in the creation of the visualizations for our data story and display the first 5 entries in these datasets.

Dataset 1 : Greenhouse gas emission in the Netherlands#

This dataset includes emissions of greenhouse gases in the Netherlands from 1990-2017 and is used in figure 4, 5 and 6. The data is ordered by emission source, where the emissions are measured in million kilograms, thus are continuous ratio values. The dataset is from Kaggle and needed to be separated by semicolon before usage.

The variables used are the population in thousands and the percentage of employment in agriculture, which are both discrete ratio values. Dataset 3 : Arable Land in the Europe#

This dataset contains data on the percentage of arable land in different European countries and is used to create figure 3. Since the dataset is used to compare against dataset 2, we only used values from the year 2017, which are the percentages of arable land in different countries, a discrete ratio value. The dataset was preprocessed from its original dataset to include only relevant European countries and can be found on The World Bank.

Dataset 4 : Global Air Pollution#

This dataset contains geolocated data on the AQI of of large cities in nearly all countries and was used in figure 1. The attributes used in this story are the AQI values of the gases N2O, CO and Ozone. The values are measured, which makes them continuous ratio values. The dataset was cleaned before usage by grouping the data by country and aggregating the temperature values. The dataset can be found on Kaggle.

Dataset 5 : Agricultural companies in the Netherlands#

This dataset includes data on agricultural companies in the Netherlands over the period of 1851-2022, and was used to create figure 6. The variables in the dataset that were used include the number of all agricultural companies and companies that hold cattle or pigs, which are discrete ratio values. The dataset was preprocessed by appending ‘JJ00’ to the years in the ‘Perioden’ column, and the columns describing companies with cattle and pigs were combined using a sum of the values. The dataset can be found at CBS.

Dataset 6 : Import and Export of the Netherlands#

This dataset describes the import and export income of goods from the Netherlands in the years 2021 and 2022. The variable ‘export value’ of the several SITC values describing agricultural products are used to make figure 7. These variables describe products from the agricultural sector, are measured in million euros and are discrete ratio values. The dataset was not preprocessed besides filtering on the SITC values and can be found at CBS.
